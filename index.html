Meditation is a practice that involves training the mind to focus and redirect thoughts. It can take many forms, such as mindfulness, transcendental meditation, loving-kindness meditation, and more. The goal is often to cultivate awareness, reduce stress, promote relaxation, and enhance overall well-being.

Mindfulness Meditation

Focuses on being present in the moment, observing thoughts, emotions, and sensations without judgment.

Transcendental Meditation

Transcendental Meditation Involves silently repeating a mantra to achieve a state of relaxed awareness.

Loving-kindness Meditation

Cultivates feelings of love and compassion towards oneself and others through the repetition of phrases.

Body Scan Meditation

Involves systematically scanning the body, bringing awareness to each part and any sensations present.

Zen Meditation

A form of seated meditation practiced in Zen Buddhism, often focusing on the breath or sitting without a specific focus.

Vipassana Meditation

Observes sensations of the body and impermanent nature of thoughts and emotions to develop insight.

Kundalini Meditation

Aims to awaken the Kundalini energy through practices like breathwork, chanting, movement, and visualization

Walking Meditation

Involves bringing mindfulness to the act of walking, focusing on sensations of movement and the environment.
